Teaching

I believe in learning by doing—and teaching by learning. As I explore the world of robotics, I share what I learn through engaging YouTube videos and thoughtful explanations. My goal is to simplify complex topics like computer vision, control systems, and human-robot interaction, so anyone curious about robotics can jump right in.
