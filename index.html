About Me

Hi! I'm Vamsi Sai Abhijit Tadepalli, a Robotics master's student at Arizona State University. My passion lies in building intelligent robotic systems that can perceive, interact, and adapt to the world around them. I’m especially excited about the intersection of computer vision, human-robot interaction, and virtual/augmented reality.

Whether it’s through hands-on research or teaching others, I’m driven by a simple goal: to make robotics more accessible, meaningful, and human-aware.
